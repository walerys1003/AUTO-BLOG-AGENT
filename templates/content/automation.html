Automation Strategy

Automation works by:

  1. Selecting approved topics with high SEO scores
  2. Generating high-quality content automatically
  3. Publishing directly to your WordPress blogs
  4. Promoting content across your social media accounts

Content is generated based on your style preferences and SEO-optimized.

Scheduling Tips
  • Schedule 1-4 posts per day
  • Choose days based on your audience activity
  • Set publishing times during peak hours
  • Vary content length for different topics
  • Auto-promote to maximize reach

Rules will only publish if enough approved topics are available.

Quality Control

Ensuring high-quality automated content:

  • Set minimum topic score thresholds
  • Filter by specific categories
  • Auto-enable topics for faster workflows
  • Configure automatic social promotion

You can pause or adjust automation rules at any time.
